About Me

GIS Developer & Data Engineer

I'm a GIS Developer and Spatial Data Engineer with deep expertise in building enterprise geospatial solutions for government and research sectors. With a Master's in Spatial Engineering from the University of Melbourne and extensive experience across local councils and research institutions, I specialize in automating complex spatial workflows, developing full-stack web mapping applications, and architecting scalable data pipelines.

Currently serving as GIS Officer at Greater Shepparton Council and Data Engineer at the Centre for Spatial Data Infrastructures and Land Administration, I bridge the gap between spatial data science and practical implementation. My work focuses on reducing manual effort through automation, improving data quality through rigorous validation, and making geospatial insights accessible to non-technical stakeholders.

I'm passionate about leveraging modern technologies—from OpenLayers and React for interactive mapping to FME and Python for data engineering—to solve real-world spatial challenges.
